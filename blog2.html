Freisan horses

Friesian horses are a tall, big-boned equine breed that typically has a black coat with thick manes and tails. These animals have the desired temperament and athletic ability to perform well in dressage. They are also suitable horses for beginners or advanced riders. Friesian horses are majestic animals with great size and color.
