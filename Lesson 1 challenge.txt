    <title>delicious and easy pasta bake recipe</title>
<p> The most simple easy and delicious pasta bake recipe. Easy cheese and tomato pasta bake for nights where you want something delicious and comforting to eat but can’t be bothered to spend ages in the kitchen. This recipe takes 50 minutes. This recipe serves 6 people. </p>

<h1>Ingredients</h1>
<ul>
    <li>2 x 400g (14 oz) cans cherry tomatoes</li>
    <li>1 red onion finely chopped</li>
    <li>3 garlic cloves thinly sliced</li>
    <li>1 sprig fresh rosemary</li>
    <li>2 sprigs fresh thyme</li>
    <li>1 teaspoon Italian herbs</li>
    <li>½ teaspoon smoked paprika optional</li>
    <li>pinch of chilli flakes optional</li>
    <li>2 tablespoons Balsamic vinegar</li>
    <li>1-2 teaspoons sugar to taste (optional)</li>
    <li>½ cup vegetable stock/red wine</li>
    <li>salt & pepper to taste</li>
    <li>optional seasoning</li>
    </ul>
<p>For the pasta bake</p>
<ul>
<li>500 g (1lb) short pasta of your choice</li>
<li>1 cup grated mozzarella cheese</li>
<li>1 cup grated cheddar cheese</li>
<li>fresh basil to serve</li>
</ul>
